About Us

Ayubowan! Traditional greetings and a warm welcome to Simpson’s Forest hotel, where a new and different luxury boutique hotel experience awaits guests in the cool climes of the Hill Country of Sri Lanka.


Pleasing design aesthetics and modern conveniences will have guests feeling right at home and ensure an unforgettable stay. The hotel is in the quiet surroundings of a tea and spice plantation more than sixty acres in extent and gets its name from a former English planter who nurtured a forest patch (Simpson’s Forest) bordering the plantation.
