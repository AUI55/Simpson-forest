Nuwaraeliya Hotel

Simpson Forest Hotel, nestled in the picturesque town of Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka, offers a serene and tranquil escape amidst the lush greenery of the surrounding landscape. This charming hotel features cozy rooms with modern amenities, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests. Surrounded by the breathtaking beauty of the mist-covered hills and tea plantations, guests can enjoy nature walks and explore the local culture. With its warm hospitality and breathtaking views, Simpson Forest Hotel promises an unforgettable experience for those seeking a peaceful retreat in the heart of Sri Lanka's hill country.
